Goals

The goal of the project was to provide an interesting way to showcase illustrated artwork.

  1. Learn how to Apply Augmented Reality (AR)
    1. Learn how Unity works with AR
    2. Learn VUFORIA
  2. Parallax Scrolling - I was really hoping to work with more parallaxing elements and to play around the project to learn the limitations of the medium.
  3. App Development - Working on and android phone

What is Augmented Reality

Augment Reality(AR) provides digital enrvonrments real world context. If you think of Virtual Reality as being a visitor in the digital world AR will brings the diginal world to us.

Unity is a cross-platform game engine, aiming to "democratize" game development by making it accessible to more developers and artist. The tool allows artist to quickly and rapidly prototype concepts , ideas and innovations.

In this case we're looking add interactivity to a playing card. Unity will be adding the ability of;

  • Camera Access
  • Android Access
  • Image and Planar Tracking

Vuforia is a company that provides artist with simple tools to create track images and to replace them with 3D models or other digital assets.

  • Image recognition
  • Planar tracking based on a target image.

Parallaxing Scrolling

Simply put Parallax is a visual phenome where objects closer to the observer appear to move faster than objects further away from the observer.

Version 1

After installing all of the software. I used my phone and a web camera to serve as the target image. I needed to learn how to properly track a target and apply 3D object's orientation to it.

Version 2

After I figured out how to track and apply 3d transformations too. I needed to apply that to a fake 3D digital card.

Version 3

With This version, I was able to split up and rework the layer order inside of Unity and Photoshop. From there I needed to add z Depth to each individual layer.

To work it out correctly I needed to learn how to mask out specifica areas and needed.

If you notice that the focus is on the clock gears. I hoping to work the parallaxing views into the foliages and trees. It was planned to work that into the foliage. However the way the the layers were painted I wasnt able to acomodate this.
